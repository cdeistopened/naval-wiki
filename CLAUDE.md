# Ask Naval — Wealth Creation & Life Philosophy Coach

An AI coach built from 160 episodes, posts, and interviews by Naval Ravikant. Not a chatbot impersonating Naval — a structured library of real frameworks, real quotes, and real decision tools extracted from his work. Every recommendation traces back to a specific source.

## Quick Start

Ask any question about wealth creation, business strategy, happiness, or AI:
- "How do I find my specific knowledge?"
- "Am I building wealth or just renting time?"
- "Should I partner with this person?"
- "Teach me about leverage"
- "How do I become happier?"
- "How should I use AI in my work?"

## What's Inside

### 21 Interactive Skills
Decision-making, teaching, and coaching frameworks that walk you through specific questions step by step. Each one asks about your situation, applies Naval's specific frameworks with his actual quotes, and delivers a structured output.

### 39 Framework Guides
Detailed written guides on specific concepts — from the four types of luck to the aspirational hourly rate to why every human is a spellcaster. Includes 4 deep-dive reference articles (1,500-2,500 words each with extensive quotes) and 2 cross-pollination articles connecting Naval's frameworks to Balaji Srinivasan and Alex Hormozi. Each guide includes specific quotes, concrete examples, and source citations. Organized across seven domains: Wealth Creation, Leverage, Startups & Investing, Philosophy & Thinking, Happiness & Peace, Society, and AI & Technology.

### 482 Searchable Chunks
Naval's full archive — 139 blog posts, 17 long-form interviews, and 4 podcast episodes — semantically chunked into 482 segments across 7 themes. Claude can search these to answer any question with direct quotes and source citations.

### Progressive Disclosure
You don't need to read 160 sources. The system works in layers:
1. **Ask a question** — Claude routes to the right skill or framework
2. **Go deeper** — Claude reads the source chunks for more context
3. **Go deepest** — Claude searches across all 482 chunks for related insights

## How It's Built

This plugin was built by the [Creative Intelligence Agency](https://creativeintel.agency), decomposing Naval's full archive into 48 frameworks using Claude Code's archive decomposer pipeline. Every framework was scored against a 5-test skill-worthiness filter. The 10 highest-scoring became decision skills. The rest became tutor skills, coaching skills, and reference articles. Every quote traces back to a specific source — nothing is invented.

Sources: [Naval](https://nav.al) by Naval Ravikant — podcast, blog, and interviews.

## Note

This is an educational tool that organizes information from public sources. It is not financial, legal, or professional advice. Naval himself would tell you to reject most advice — including his.
